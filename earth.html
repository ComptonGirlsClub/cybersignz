Earth Signz

The Earth Signs are nurturing and have a lot of stability. The Earth element is composed of three signs: Virgo’s, Tauruses, and Capricornians. They're all known to be practical, reliable, honest, and real. Though the signs may seem very uptight when you first meet them, they’re very diligent people to be around.
