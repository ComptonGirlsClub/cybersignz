Fire sSignz

Fire signs are the most passionate and adventurous of all the elements. The fire element is composed of three fire signs: Aries, Leos, and Sagittariuses. They are bold, loyal, beautiful and they don’t mind being the center of attention. They love to tell it how it is and their go-to colors are bold like themselves: hot pink, red, etc

Interview Question 1

What does your sign mean to you?

Being a sagittarius means being outgoing and having a good sense of humor.

Interview Question 2

What fashion style matches your element?

She wears whatever she wants because she doesn't care what people think.

Interview Question 3

Do you think fashion helps motivate you? (Helps boost your self esteem, etc)

Fashion makes her feel good because sometimes she changes her outfit just to look at herself in the mirror. To reassure herself that she is who she is and that she’s the baddest.
